Live Well, Love Much, Laugh Often

Depot is not only the name of the restaurant where Chef Michael Shafer is the iron horse and engineer, it's the creative hub of culinary lines from all over the world that have marked his career, from Classical cuisine to German, Austrian, contemporary Scandinavian, pan-Asian, and Mexican to American cooking from the East Coast to the Southwest and California.

At Depot we smoke our own meats, make our own sausages, and our bread rises to your table. And don't forget to save room for our fresh pastries, ice creams and desserts.

Depot is available for private parties, meetings, and toga parties. Ask for our Party Brochure or phone for a consultation.

Learn to cook, the Shafer way... at the Chef's fun monthly cooking classes. Sign up today, they always sell out!
